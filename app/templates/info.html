About

This nationwide database of pre and primary schools in Tanzania was built through the collaboration between The World Food Programme (WFP) in partnership and support from the Government of Tanzania through President’s Office Regional Administration and Local Government (PO-RALG) and the Ministry of Education, Science and Technology (MoET).

The webmap visualizes basic information on pre and primary schools in Tanzania then deep dives into complex analyses that provide insights such as whether the schools have feeding program systems in place to provide meals to students during school hours, and if yes, types of meals provided, at what intervals, etc. Furthermore, the webmap highlights data about other school-feeding related affairs such as whether the schools have access to electricity or other energy-source types, school cooking infrastructures, presence of locally produced cereal foods and their types, etc.

The webmap is valuable for decision-makers—government and stakeholders in school feeding programs— to better understand gaps and opportunities in the school feeding program and facilitate the design of evidence-based interventions. It provides users with ability to filter data for viewing as well as to download filtered data and infographics for further analyses according to needs.

Data was collected, curated and web-visualized by OpenMap Development Tanzania.
